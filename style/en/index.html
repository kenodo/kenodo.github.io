About me

To me fashion is not only a form of art, but a daily possibility to express yourself - the mood you have today, the message you want to send to the outta world. Express yourself on daily basis is the truest courage one can have & and a way to achieve your inner balance / self- confidence. Feeling yourself comfortable, confident, beautiful & stylish on daily basis is one of the possibilities - the choice is yours.

Services

Fashion stylist : Styling photoshoots for magazines / online magazines / websites / special projects / brand campaigns ( beforehand & on- site)

Personal shopping : Conscious shopping : best deals / special discounts in various shops / re- organizing & re-adjusting your wardrobe and much more

Style consultation & relooking : introductory recommendations regarding your style / color palette / shape type + a special list of stores in your city / online shops that have a suitable range of clothing / accessories for you and your wallet!

Wardrobe styling : Before heading to a shopping mall and waisting money again, have a fresh look at your wardrobe. (with my personal help) It will definitely surprise you!
